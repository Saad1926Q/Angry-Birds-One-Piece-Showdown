Gomu Gomu No Launch: One Piece Showdown

About this Game

Inspired by the beloved Angry Birds franchise, I've embarked on a creative journey to reimagine its magic through Matter.js. Utilizing Matter.js's robust physics engine, I've meticulously crafted a dynamic game environment where players can relive the excitement of launching Luffy in his Gum-Gum Balloon form to overcome intricate challenges. Instead of a traditional slingshot mechanic, players engage in intuitive mouse-driven interactions, dragging and releasing Luffy to topple elaborate structures and outwit iconic adversaries from the One Piece anime. With meticulous attention to physics realism, I've fine-tuned parameters such as gravity, restitution, and friction to deliver an authentic gaming experience reminiscent of the original. From designing captivating levels filled with ingenious obstacles to implementing seamless user controls, every element of this project embodies the spirit of the iconic classic. Through this endeavor, I aim to evoke nostalgia while offering a fresh, immersive take on the timeless joy of Angry Birds, all made possible by the versatility and power of Matter.js. Join Luffy on his epic adventure, battling enemies in the sequence of the original anime, and experience the thrill of Gomu Gomu No Launch: One Piece Showdown!

User Guide

Objective: Your objective is to defeat the mischievous adversaries by strategically launching birds to topple their structures.

Controls:Launch Luffy: Click and drag on the player to set its trajectory and release to launch.
